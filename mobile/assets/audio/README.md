# Audio Assets

Place your audio files in this directory. The following files are expected:

## Sound Effects (SFX)
- `jump.mp3` - Player jump sound (short, snappy)
- `land.mp3` - Player landing sound (soft thud)
- `coin.mp3` - Coin collection sound (bright, rewarding)
- `bounce.mp3` - Bounce platform sound (springy)
- `death.mp3` - Player death sound (short, dramatic)
- `complete.mp3` - Level complete fanfare
- `button.mp3` - UI button press sound

## Background Music (Optional)
- `menu.mp3` - Main menu background music
- `game.mp3` - In-game background music
- `endless.mp3` - Endless mode music (more intense)

## Recommended Specifications
- Format: MP3 or M4A
- Sample Rate: 44.1kHz
- Bit Rate: 128-192 kbps for music, 64-128 kbps for SFX
- Duration: SFX should be < 1 second, music can loop

## Free Resources

- https://incompetech.com - Free music by Kevin MacLeod
- https://opengameart.org - Game audio assets
